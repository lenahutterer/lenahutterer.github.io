Assignment 1 - Application Comparison

Application Comparison

For this comparison the two Apps that have been chosen are "Whatsapp" and "Telegram". Both serve the same purpose of being a communication application for primarily text messaging.

Both applications offer a web-based version that can easily be accessed through a web browser. In order to do so you simply have to scan the QR code that is provided on the computer screen through the App on your phone. I knew how to do it on Whatsapp since I have been using it for several years now as opposed to Telegram which I have only been using for a couple of months now - but they surprisingly worked exactly identical - this made it very easy for me to figure it out!

Whatsapp

Different Activities within the App

1. The Chat Function: you have a list of your different chats to either a single person or a group-chat. Whithin this menu it is also possible to start a new chat.

2. The Calls Function: you can have calls through this app with either audio only or even video. Other than calling someone over phone as you would usually, you can call someone using only wifi or mobile data.

3. The Status Function: similar to Instagram, Facebook, Snapchat, etc. you can post some sort of a "story" where you share a photo with some text on your profile for everyone to view it. There is even a function to reply or react to it.

4. The Camera Function: by clicking on the camera icon you can take a photo and then choose to whom you want to send it.

Functionality through Control Tools

What the user can controll & change:

1. I can change my appearance in terms of changing my profile picture, my name that appears to the chat-partners, my phone number and my "caption" or "about".

2. I can choose which devices I want to link to my account. This can either be another phone, my laptop application or the web based application.

3. I can choose whether to back-up my chats, or to have it automatically done; move them to another phone (even from iphone to android); archive them; clear them from my phone or delete them all.

4. I can manage my storage. I can choose some of the above options to do so or get an insight on the usage that is being displayed for me; I can also choose to use less data for calls and if I want the app to use my Cellular or Wifi only.

Comparison

Progressive Disclosure?

To my mind, both of these two Apps have a very progressive disclosure. Both show what is most relevant which is the "chats" and the less relevant options are hidden away behind "icons" either on the bottom in a bar or in the corners of the screen. These icons also symbolize a "group" of actions, in that way the user knows exactly where to find what he/she needs and knows where to look for it even when he/she is unsure.

Authorization Process

The Authorization Processs for the phone application is a little bit different between these apps. For Whatsapp one has to put in their phone number which is a huge deal when it comes to privacy especially when it is also connected with your name. Telegram on the other hand has the advantage that it operated without phone numbers but simply with an online user profile which is especially appealing for either business use or usage for big groups like university sports clubs where there are often over 300 people from all over the world.

For loging into the web-based application the user simply has to scan the QR code that is displayed on the computer screen with the phone through the app and is automatically logged in.
